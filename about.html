About Us

Bikini is a two-piece swimsuit typically worn by women, consisting of two parts: a top and a bottom.
Bikinies are typically worn for beach outings, pool parties, or sunbathing, offering a balance of style and functionality for water activities and lounging.
Made from materials like nylon, spandex, or polyester, the fabric is stretchy, lightweight, and quick-drying. Many bikinis feature lining for added comfort and modesty.
Bikinis come in a wide variety of patterns, colors, and prints, from solid colors to florals, stripes, or tropical prints.
The level of coverage also varies, ranging from more modest styles like boyshorts or briefs to more revealing cuts like Brazilian or thong bottoms.

Our Mission

At Bikini Dress Matter, our mission is to empower women to feel confident, stylish, and comfortable in their own skin through our thoughtfully designed swimwear. We are committed to creating bikinis that celebrate diverse body.

Our Vision

Body Positivity: We believe that every body is a bikini body. Our designs cater to all shapes and sizes, promoting confidence and self-expression.
Sustainability: We are dedicated to eco-friendly practices by using sustainable materials.

Our History

Bikini Dress Matter was founded in 2015 by a group of women passionate about swimwear and fashion. Noticing a gap in the market for high-quality, inclusive bikinis, they set out to create a brand that offered designs for all body.

Our Ethical Practices

We are committed to upholding the highest ethical standards across all aspects of our work. Our practices ensure that we maintain the integrity, privacy, and consent of all individuals involved.

1. Consent

We ensure that all participants provide informed consent before being involved in any of our activities. This includes the right to withdraw consent at any time without any repercussions.

2. Privacy

We are dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users and participants. Any data collected is handled with strict confidentiality and used only for the purposes explicitly stated during consent.

3. Transparency

We provide clear and transparent information about how we collect, store, and use data. Participants are informed about the data usage policies and their rights regarding their personal information.

4. Data Protection

We employ advanced security measures to safeguard any personal information shared with us. We adhere to legal requirements and industry standards for data protection and privacy.

5. Ethical Conduct

All our activities are conducted with integrity, fairness, and respect for the individuals and communities involved. We avoid any form of bias, discrimination, or exploitation in our processes.
